Spicy Basil Chicken

A quick and flavorful Thai-style dish with chicken, basil, garlic, and chili.

30 minutes Easy 4 servings

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Thai basil
  • Garlic
  • Chili
  • Soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Prepare all ingredients.
  2. Stir-fry garlic and chili until fragrant.
  3. Add chicken and cook until done.
  4. Add seasoning and basil.
  5. Serve with rice.
